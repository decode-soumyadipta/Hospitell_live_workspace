Basic Questions
1. What information do I need to provide before booking a doctor appointment?

You are required to provide thorough information about your health records, medical condition, current medication, and so forth. If you are referred to the hospital by a doctor in your residing area, it is recommended to bring along the contact details of the doctor to your scheduled appointment.

2. How can I book a medical appointment?

You can schedule an appointment at to schedule a medical appointment or Online Consultation at and learn more about the hospitals. Alternatively, you can fill in the form at our Reach Us section.

3. How can I get queue number of my doctor appointment?

Once you have booked a medical appointment, you will be assigned a queue number based on parameters like emergency quota, senior citizen and first in first out.
